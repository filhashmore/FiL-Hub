## Featured Projects

- **[RF Scout](https://rf-scout.vercel.app)** — RF frequency coordination tool for touring audio professionals. Real-time spectrum visualization, FCC API integration, IM product avoidance, export to Shure WWB & Sennheiser WSM. *Beta*

- **[SetFlow](https://setflow-beryl.vercel.app)** — Setlist builder with BPM/key metadata, Circle of Fifths key clash detection, energy flow analysis, and stage-optimized PDF export. *Beta*

- **[Filmore Advance Portal](https://filmore-advance.vercel.app)** — Self-service venue advance portal. Venues access tech riders, stage plots, and production docs. Access codes: DEMO, FLY1, TRIO. *Beta*

- **[Tour Advance Portal](https://tour-advance-portal.vercel.app)** — Config-driven, multi-tenant advance portal template for any production manager. *In Development*

- **[Filmore Gear Inventory](https://filmore-gear.vercel.app)** — Touring gear tracking with consumables management and fly pack assignments. Access code: DEMO4. *Beta*

- **[Filmore 1-Sheet](https://filmore-1sheet.vercel.app)** — Digital press kit for FILMORE featuring 481M+ streams and Spotify demographics. *Active*

## About FiL Hash

FiL Hash brings 15 years of touring music industry experience to software development — spanning FOH & monitor engineering, production management, stage management, and playback. With a parallel career in photography, visual design, and branding for performing artists, FiL builds web and mobile tools that solve real production workflow problems — from RF coordination to setlist planning to venue advances.
